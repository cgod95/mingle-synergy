"Hi [Venue Name], I'm [Your Name] from Mingle. We're building a new way for people to meet at venues like yours. We're looking for a venue partner for our Beta 2 test, and I think [Venue Name] would be perfect."

### The Problem
"Dating apps are broken - people swipe endlessly but rarely meet. We believe people want to meet in person, at real places like yours."

### The Solution
"Mingle lets people check into venues, see who else is there, and match with people they're interested in. It's focused on real connections at real places."

"For Beta 2, we're looking for one venue partner. We'll bring 20-30 people to your venue for one event, and you'll get:
- Increased foot traffic
- Customer insights via our analytics dashboard
- Promotion in our app and social media

### The Ask
"We're asking for:
- Permission to test at your venue

- Optional: Host a 'Mingle Night' event"

- Discuss partnership terms
- Plan the Beta 2 event
- Set up analytics dashboard"
